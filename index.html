Matias Rodriguez Sfeir is a writer-director born and raised in Mexico City. He studied film & television at Centro de Diseño, Cine y Television before attending London Film School for a masters in screenwriting.

His thesis film "Plumaje" (2023) was screened at Morelia International Film Festival and Brussels Short Film Festival.
